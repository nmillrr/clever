# Academic Access Extension Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: June 13, 2025*

## Introduction

Academic Access Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

The Academic Access Extension collects and stores the following information:

1. **Institution Settings**: We store your selected institution and proxy configuration locally on your device.

2. **Access History**: When you use the extension to access an article, we store a record of the article metadata (title, DOI, URL) and timestamp locally on your device.

3. **Technical Information**: Basic information about your browser environment may be collected for error reporting purposes.

The extension does NOT collect:
- Personal identifying information
- Login credentials
- Browsing history outside of academic publisher sites
- Payment information

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

1. **To Provide Core Functionality**: Institution settings are necessary to redirect you to your library's proxy server.

2. **To Improve User Experience**: Access history is used to provide a list of previously accessed articles for your convenience.

3. **For Debugging**: Technical information is used solely to identify and fix issues with the extension.

## Data Storage and Sharing

1. **Local Storage**: All user data is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs. Your data does not leave your device except as described below.

2. **Third-Party Services**:
   - **Unpaywall API**: When searching for open access alternatives, article DOIs are sent to the Unpaywall API.
   - **Error Reporting**: If you opt-in to error reporting, anonymous error logs may be sent to our monitoring service.

3. **No Data Selling**: We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to any third party.

## Data Retention

1. **User Control**: You can clear all stored data at any time through the extension options page.

2. **Automatic Deletion**: No data is retained on our servers, as all data is stored locally on your device.

## Your Choices and Rights

1. **Access History**: You can view, export, or delete your access history from the extension options page.

2. **Error Reporting**: Error reporting is opt-in and can be disabled at any time.

3. **Complete Removal**: Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
